Microinteraction: Ringer Toggle

Riley Mitchell • IDM241

INTERACTION

Platform
Mobile – iOS (iPhone)
Feature
Turning the ringer on/off
Description
The ringer can be turned on or off by clicking the bell icon button to control the ringer's status. When the bell is red and has a line through it, Silent Mode is on and the device will not ring. When the bell is white and not crossed out, the ringer is on.

TRIGGERS

  • Ringer/silent button
    • First click — Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • Second click — Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • Hover ON/OFF button

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background circle switches to white
    • The bell icon turns red
    • A diagonal slash draws in from the upper-left toward the lower-right
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly
  • Second click: Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The circle background switches to dark grey/green
    • The bell turns white
    • The diagonal slash retracts/erases
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly

RINGER / SILENT

INTERACTION

Feature
Turning the ringer on/off

TRIGGERS

  • Ringer/silent button
    • First click — Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • Second click — Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • Hover ON/OFF button

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background circle switches to white
    • The bell icon turns red
    • A diagonal slash draws in from the upper-left toward the lower-right
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly
  • Second click: Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The circle background switches to dark grey/green
    • The bell turns white
    • The diagonal slash retracts/erases
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly

FEEDBACK

  • Second click: Ringer OFF → Ringer ON

INTERACTION

Feature

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Light Mode → Dark Mode
    • On click the entire icon slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The outer circle turns white
    • The center circle turns black
    • The sun icon spins out
    • The moon icon spins in and scales up
  • Second click: Dark Mode → Light Mode
    • On click the entire icon slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The outer circle turns green/gray
    • The center circle turns white
    • The moon icon spins out
    • The sun icon spins in and scales back

LOW POWER MODE

INTERACTION

Feature
Turning Low Power Mode on/off

TRIGGERS

  • Low Power button
    • First click — Low Power OFF → Low Power ON
    • Second click — Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
    • Hover ON/OFF button

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Low Power OFF → Low Power ON
    • The outer circle changes from dark grey/green to white
    • The battery outline and tip change to yellow
    • The inner charge bar expands to a longer yellow bar
  • Second click: Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
    • The outer circle changes from white back to dark grey/green
    • The battery outline and tip change back to white/grey
    • The inner charge bar shrinks back to the smaller white bar

FEEDBACK

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Low Power OFF → Low Power ON
    • The outer circle flashes to white
    • The battery turns yellow
    • The charge bar grows to show more power
  • Second click: Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
    • The outer circle fades back to dark grey/green
    • The battery returns to the white/grey style
    • The charge bar shrinks back to the shorter bar

LOOPS & MODES

    None

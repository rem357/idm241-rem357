RINGER / SILENT

INTERACTION

Platform
Mobile – iOS (iPhone)
Feature
Turning the ringer on/off in Control Center

TRIGGERS

  • Ringer/silent button
    • First click — Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • Second click — Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • Hover ON/OFF button

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Ringer ON → Ringer OFF
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background circle switches to white
    • The bell icon turns red
    • A diagonal slash draws in from the upper-left toward the lower-right
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly
  • Second click: Ringer OFF → Ringer ON
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The circle background switches to dark grey/green
    • The bell turns white
    • The diagonal slash retracts/erases
    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly

FEEDBACK

    • The bell icon wiggles/shakes briefly
  • Second click: Ringer OFF → Ringer ON

RULES

    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Light Mode → Dark Mode
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background turns white
    • The icon rotates
    • The center circle grows slightly and then shrinks back
    • All white parts turn black
    • All green/gray parts turn white
  • Second click: Dark Mode → Light Mode
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background turns green/gray
    • The icon rotates back

    • All white parts turn green/gray

LOW POWER MODE

INTERACTION

Platform
Mobile – iOS (iPhone)
Feature
Turning Low Power Mode on/off in Control Center

TRIGGERS

  • Low Power button
    • First click — Low Power OFF → Low Power ON
    • Second click — Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
    • Hover ON/OFF button

RULES

  • Hover ON
    • Cursor changes to finger
  • Hover OFF
    • Finger changes to cursor
  • First click: Low Power OFF → Low Power ON
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background circle switches to dark grey/green
    • The battery icon turns yellow
    • The inner charge bar briefly sweeps and then settles
  • Second click: Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
    • On click the entire button slightly grows in size and then shrinks
    • The background circle switches to white
    • The battery icon returns to a white outline
    • The inner charge bar fades out

  • Second click: Low Power ON → Low Power OFF
